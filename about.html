Who Am I?

Thanks for visiting my website. My name is Isaac Bratcher. I'm a dual-enrolled student, currently working towards a software engineering degree at OCC. Obviously, one of my hobbies is messing around with Rubik's cubes. But I also build games in Unity, even participating in Game Jams. I have high expectations for myself, and my plans for the future. So stay in touch and expect great things!





My Experience

My experience is honestly all over the spectrum. I have coordinated events with over 3000 active participants, moderating that same community and making sure everything goes smoothly. I'm also an administrator of Neurodivergent of FIRST. Here we give a voice to those who might be too afraid to speak for themselves within robotics. But speaking of robotics, I have been the lead programmer of 7211 Team Hollywood for 4 years in a row. I'm also the Vice President of iTeam, a student led organization at OSTC.
